Technology-Based Hiring Improvements

1. AI & Automation for Resume Screening

  • Implement an AI-driven ATS (Applicant Tracking System) for automated resume parsing and keyword-based screening.

  • Leverage Chatbots for candidate engagement (e.g., answering FAQs about FIS, interview scheduling).

2. Skill-Based Assessments & Online Testing

  • Use platforms like HackerRank, Codility, or Mettl for technical assessments.
  • Introduce AI-driven coding challenges to filter top talent.
  • Utilize behavioral and psychometric tests to assess cultural fitment.

3. Data-Driven Hiring Decisions

  • Implement predictive analytics in hiring to assess which candidates are more likely to succeed.
  • Use past hiring data to identify trends in candidate success and rejections.
  • Integrate LinkedIn Talent Insights to analyze talent market trends.

4. Streamlined Interview Process with Digital Tools

  • Introduce structured interview templates in ATS to avoid redundant questions.
